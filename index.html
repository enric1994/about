About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student funded by the Marie Skłodowska-Curie foundation at Dublin City University advised by Professor Noel O'Connor and Doctor Kevin McGuiness.
I received my B.Sc in Telecommunications Engineering at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya in 2018.

My research lies in the intersection between AI and 3D animation, with a focus on generative adversarial networks.
